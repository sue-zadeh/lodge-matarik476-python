{# ============ ABOUT / OUR STORY ============ #}

Who we are

Lodge Matariki 476 is part of Freemasonry in New Zealand. We bring together members from different backgrounds who share values of integrity, respect, and service.

Our meetings combine tradition with modern life – a place to pause, connect, and do something meaningful for others.

{# ============ THREE PILLARS / “GOOD VIBES” STYLE ============ #}

What you’ll find here

Becoming a Freemason isn’t about secret handshakes – it’s about friendship, personal growth, and helping others.

Whether you’re curious, new to the craft, or a visiting brother, you’ll find a warm welcome at Matariki.

Friendship

Genuine connections and support, inside and outside the lodge room.

Charity

Quietly helping our local community in practical ways.

Tradition

Ritual, symbolism, and values passed down through generations.
